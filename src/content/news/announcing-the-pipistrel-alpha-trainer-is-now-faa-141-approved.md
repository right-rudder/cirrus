## Experience Innovation with the Pipistrel Alpha Trainer

At Cirrus Aviation, we’re excited to announce that the Pipistrel Alpha Trainer has officially received FAA Part 141 approval, making it the latest addition to our fleet and a top choice for students seeking high-performance, cost-effective flight training.

This new certification enhances our ability to offer comprehensive training programs, ensuring that our students have access to the latest and most efficient aircraft available.

## Why the Pipistrel Alpha Trainer is Perfect for FAA 141 Training

The Pipistrel Alpha Trainer is designed to deliver the ideal balance between advanced technology and ease of use, making it an excellent option for flight schools and students. Here’s why it stands out:

- Technically Advanced Aircraft (TAA): The Pipistrel Alpha Trainer qualifies as a TAA, fulfilling the FAA’s 10-hour requirement for the Commercial Pilot License (CPL).
- FAA Part 141 Certification: The Alpha Trainer has passed rigorous FAA evaluations and is now fully certified for Part 141 flight training, ensuring compliance with all industry standards for quality training.
- User-Friendly for All Skill Levels: The Alpha Trainer’s intuitive controls and simplicity make it ideal for students just starting their aviation journey, while still offering the performance required by more experienced pilots.

## Key Features of the Pipistrel Alpha Trainer

The Pipistrel Alpha Trainer is equipped with cutting-edge avionics and features designed to enhance the learning experience:

- Garmin G3X Touch Glass Cockpit: The G3X Touch is a high-resolution, multi-function display that integrates key flight data, offering pilots access to essential information with just a glance. With its touchscreen interface, pilots can easily interact with the system, reducing workload and improving situational awareness.

- Synthetic Vision Technology: The Alpha Trainer is equipped with Synthetic Vision, which provides a real-time 3D representation of terrain and obstacles, enhancing safety and navigation during flight.
- Lightweight, Durable Construction: Built for training, the Alpha Trainer’s lightweight design enhances both fuel efficiency and flight handling, while its durability ensures a long service life with minimal maintenance.
