As the sun dipped below the horizon, painting the Sarasota sky in hues of orange and pink, our fleet stood proudly on the tarmac—a testament to three decades of excellence in aviation training. This breathtaking moment, captured by our talented student pilot, Tim, encapsulates not just the beauty of flight but also the passion that has driven Cirrus Aviation for the past 30 years.

![Airplanes lined up with the sunset and airplane hangars in the background](/media/blog/sunset-hangar-30-year-anniversary-cirrus-aviation-flight-school-sarasota.png)

Since our inception in 1994, Cirrus Aviation has been dedicated to producing the most professional and skilled aviators in the industry. Our commitment to personalized flight training is what sets us apart. We don’t believe in a one-size-fits-all approach. Instead, we tailor each student’s training experience to their unique strengths, motivations, and learning styles, ensuring they reach their full potential.

## Why Choose Cirrus Aviation?

Whether you’re aspiring to become an airline pilot or simply want to explore the skies for fun, Cirrus Aviation offers a range of programs designed to meet your needs:

- FAA Part 141 Flight School: Our rigorous standards ensure top-notch training and aircraft safety, with approved programs for Private Pilot, Instrument Rating, Commercial Pilot, Certified Flight Instructor, and more.
- VISA Approved: We welcome international students, offering comprehensive support from visa processing to English language proficiency.
- Liberty University Affiliate: Train locally in Sarasota while earning an Aeronautics degree online from a prestigious university.
- Proven Ground School Curriculum: We use the Cessna/King Schools Ground School Curriculum, renowned for its quality and convenience.

## A Legacy of Excellence

Located at Sarasota-Bradenton International Airport, Cirrus Aviation has proudly trained pilots from Sarasota, Clearwater, St. Petersburg, Tampa, and beyond. Our mission is simple: to provide safe, thorough training in a professional yet friendly environment. Over the years, we’ve had the privilege of helping countless students achieve their aviation dreams, whether flying for recreation or pursuing a career in the skies.
