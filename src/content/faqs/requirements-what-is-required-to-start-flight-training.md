---
question: "What are the requirements to start flight training?"
category: "Requirements"
order: 2
---

In order to start training for a private pilot license, the base certification for flight training, you must:
- Be at least 17 years old.
- Be proficient in English.

> **Note:** Before you can fly solo you must also hold at least a Third-Class Medical Certificate. An FAA medical exam by an Aviation Medical Examiner (AME) is required.
